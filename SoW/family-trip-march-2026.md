## PRACTICAL NOTES

### Getting to/from Stansted
- **Stansted Express** train to Liverpool Street: ~47 min, from £19.80 (book advance for cheaper)
- **National Express coach**: From £10, takes ~60–90 min to Victoria
- **With kids:** Consider the coach — cheaper and less stressful with luggage

### Barcelona Airport → La Rambla
- **Aerobús** (airport bus): €7.75 single / €13.65 return per adult, kids under 4 free
- Takes 35 min to Plaça Catalunya (top of La Rambla)
- Runs every 5 min

### Luggage
- Ryanair fares above are **cabin bag only** (small personal bag included)
- 10kg cabin bag: ~£/€13 per person per flight
- 20kg check-in bag: ~£/€25–35 per person per flight
- **Tip:** For a family, consider 1–2 checked bags rather than per-person cabin bags

### Things to Do with Kids (9 & 11)
**Barcelona:**
- La Rambla / La Boqueria market (free to walk, tastings)
- Barceloneta Beach
- Sagrada Familia (book tickets in advance — ~€26/adult, under 11 free)
- Park Güell (free areas available, monumental zone ~€10/adult)
- Barcelona Aquarium (Port Vell, near La Rambla)
- CosmoCaixa Science Museum

**London:**
- Natural History Museum (FREE!)
- Science Museum (FREE!)
- Tower of London (~£33/adult, kids ~£16)
- South Bank walk / London Eye area
- British Museum (FREE!)
- Greenwich — Cutty Sark, Observatory
